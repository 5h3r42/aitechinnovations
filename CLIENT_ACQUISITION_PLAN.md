# 30-Day Client Acquisition Campaign

## Goal

Generate four qualified strategy calls, two proposals and one paying UK service-business client in the first 30-day cycle.

## Target Accounts

Build a list of 100 UK service businesses with a visible website or enquiry-system problem. Prioritise clinics, professional firms, trades, property businesses and care providers that can benefit from a £399-£1,500 first project.

Record:

- Business name, location and service
- Owner or decision-maker name
- Website and contact route
- One positioning or trust issue
- One conversion or contact issue
- One follow-up, booking or automation opportunity
- Contact date, follow-up dates and outcome

## Daily Activity

- Research and contact 10-15 qualified prospects each working day.
- Send a short, personalised three-point review rather than a generic agency pitch.
- Offer the free strategy call and mention the two discounted paid pilot places when relevant.
- Follow up after three days and seven days.
- Stop after the second follow-up unless the prospect replies or gives permission to continue.

## Outreach Message

Subject: Three ideas for [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

I reviewed [website/business] and noticed three practical opportunities:

1. [Specific website, content or trust issue]
2. [Specific enquiry, booking or tracking issue]
3. [Specific follow-up, advertising or automation opportunity]

AITech Innovations builds websites, content, advertising setup and enquiry automation for UK service businesses. I have two discounted paid pilot places available while building the agency's first case studies.

Would a free 30-minute strategy call be useful? There is no obligation, and I will outline the most useful first step even if we do not work together.

Sheraz Khalid
AITech Innovations
https://www.aitechinnovations.com/free-strategy-call

## Follow-Ups

Day 3: Ask whether the three observations were relevant and offer to explain the highest-priority item on a short call.

Day 7: Send a final note with one concrete suggestion and the strategy-call link. Do not manufacture urgency or claim results that do not exist.

## Paid Pilot Terms

- The project is discounted, not free.
- Scope, price, timeline and deliverables are agreed before work begins.
- The client gives honest feedback after delivery.
- Screenshots, quotes and before/after information are published only with written permission.
- No performance result is promised or published without evidence.

## Weekly Review

Track prospects contacted, replies, positive replies, calls booked, calls attended, proposals sent, projects won and revenue. Review which sector, service pillar and message produces the strongest qualified response, then use that evidence to focus the next cycle.
