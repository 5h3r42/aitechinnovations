FAQ

Common questions before starting.

Do I need all three services?

No. Start with the current bottleneck. A combined system is only recommended when the parts genuinely need to work together.

Do you manage advertising every month?

The initial offer covers setup and launch. Ongoing campaign management is not included unless agreed separately.

Can you write the website content?

Yes. Website and landing-page projects include practical copy support based on accurate information supplied by the business.

Is the strategy call free?

Yes. It is a focused 20-minute conversation with no obligation to proceed. You will leave with one practical next step.

Why are the portfolio examples labelled as samples?

AITech Innovations is a new agency. Sample systems demonstrate capability without pretending they are completed client projects.

Who owns the website?

You own the agreed website, content and project assets. Ownership, access and handover are confirmed as part of the scope.

Is hosting included?

Hosting is scoped separately so you know exactly what is included. We can work with your existing host or agree a suitable launch setup before the project starts.

Is there ongoing support?

The initial project includes testing, launch and handover. Ongoing support or management can be agreed separately if you need it.

Are there long-term contracts?

No long-term contract is required for the initial project. Any ongoing work is agreed separately and clearly.

What happens after launch?

We test the contact routes and tracking, hand over the agreed assets and confirm the next practical step for your business.
