Privacy

Privacy Policy

AITech Innovations Ltd collects the information you choose to submit through our website, such as your name, business name, email address, phone number, website URL, project details, chatbot messages, and AI audit requests.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, discuss project requirements, deliver services, handle AI audit requests, improve enquiry workflows, and keep basic business records.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use trusted service providers such as hosting, email, analytics, form tools, calendar tools, and AI service providers where needed to operate the website and respond to enquiries.

Cookies, analytics and advertising measurement

Optional analytics and advertising technologies are disabled by default until you make a choice in the cookie banner. If you accept, Google Analytics helps us understand visits, campaign attribution and completed enquiry actions, while Microsoft Clarity helps us understand how pages are used. Google and Microsoft may process device, browser, interaction and approximate location information under their own privacy terms.

Your choice is stored in your browser so the website can remember it. Use the Cookie settings button at the bottom of any page to accept, reject or change your choice. Rejecting optional cookies does not prevent you from using forms, WhatsApp, email or booking links.

Advertising campaign information

When you arrive from an advert, we may store campaign labels such as source, medium, campaign name and a Google click identifier for the current browser session. If you submit an enquiry, those labels may be attached to the lead so we can understand which campaign generated it. We do not send form names, email addresses, phone numbers or message contents to Google Analytics.

Retention

We keep enquiry and project information only for as long as needed for business, accounting, legal, or support purposes.
