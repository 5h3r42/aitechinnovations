# Cold Email

## Purpose

Start a relevant, permission-based conversation using a real, specific observation. Personalise every message and follow applicable privacy, marketing, and platform rules.

## Template

**Subject:** `[Relevant, concise subject]`

Hi `[First name]`,

I noticed `[specific, factual website or customer-journey observation]` while looking at `[Business name]`.

For a `[industry]` business, this can make it harder for potential customers to `[desired action]`.

I have put together a short idea for improving `[relevant area]`. Would you be open to a brief conversation about it?

Best,

`[Sender name]`

## Record before sending

- Source and observation verified: `[Yes / No]`
- Contact route appropriate: `[Yes / No]`
- Prospect record updated: `[Yes / No]`
