# Prospecting Checklist

- [ ] Target business fits a priority industry and location.
- [ ] Website, services, and public contact route have been reviewed.
- [ ] A specific, factual observation has been recorded.
- [ ] The recommended service or demo is genuinely relevant.
- [ ] The business has not already asked not to be contacted.
- [ ] The prospect is added to `docs/CRM/prospect-tracker.csv`.
- [ ] A client/prospect record is created in `docs/CLIENTS/` where appropriate.
- [ ] Outreach copy is personalised and approved before sending.
- [ ] Follow-up date and owner are recorded.
