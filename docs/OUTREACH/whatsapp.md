# WhatsApp Outreach

Use WhatsApp only where the number has been provided for business contact and the approach is appropriate. Keep messages brief, relevant, and easy to decline.

## Template

Hi `[First name]`, this is `[Sender name]` from AITech Innovations. I came across `[Business name]` and noticed `[specific, factual observation]` on the website.

I have a short idea that may make it easier for customers to `[desired action]`. Would you like me to send it over?

## If there is no response

Do not repeatedly message. Record the contact attempt and follow the agreed follow-up policy.

- Date and time: `[YYYY-MM-DD]`
- Consent or contact basis: `[Notes]`
- Message sent: `[Summary]`
