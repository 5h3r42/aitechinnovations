# Sales Documentation

This directory is the working library for AITech Innovations sales, conversion-rate optimisation (CRO), outreach, customer relationship management (CRM), and client records. Keep it factual, reusable, and free of unapproved client claims or personal data.

## Where documentation belongs

- `CRO/` — website audit notes, prioritised optimisation plans, and experiment records.
- `SALES/` — repeatable sales process material: discovery, proposals, pricing, objections, onboarding, and follow-up.
- `INDUSTRIES/` — sector-specific research and sales positioning. Add evidence-backed observations and links to relevant demos.
- `OUTREACH/` — approved outreach frameworks, channel guidance, and prospecting checks.
- `CRM/` — pipeline definitions and the master prospect-tracker CSV. Update it after meaningful prospect activity.
- `CLIENTS/` — one Markdown record per prospect or client, using the structure in `CLIENTS/README.md`.

## Working principles

- Use placeholders until information has been verified.
- Record observations and outcomes clearly; do not invent client details, results, or approvals.
- Keep contact details and prospect activity in the appropriate client record and tracker.
- Keep reusable copy in `SALES/` or `OUTREACH/`; keep business-specific information in `CLIENTS/`.
