# Client Onboarding

## Onboarding checklist

- [ ] Confirmed scope, investment, payment terms, and start date.
- [ ] Named client contact and decision-maker recorded.
- [ ] Proposal or agreement stored in the client record.
- [ ] Kick-off meeting scheduled.
- [ ] Required access, assets, and brand information requested.
- [ ] Goals, priority services, and conversion actions confirmed.
- [ ] Delivery plan, responsibilities, and review dates shared.
- [ ] Communication channel and update cadence agreed.
