# Discovery Call Script

## Before the call

- Review the prospect record, website observations, and any previous messages.
- Confirm the purpose of the call and prepare only relevant examples or demos.

## Opening

"Thanks for making the time. I would like to understand how the business is attracting and converting enquiries today, share a few relevant observations, and then agree whether there is a useful next step. Does that work for you?"

## Discovery questions

1. What prompted you to take this call now?
2. What service or customer type is most important to you?
3. How do most new customers currently find you?
4. What happens when someone visits your website or contacts you?
5. What is working well, and what feels frustrating or underperforming?
6. What would a successful outcome look like?
7. Who else needs to be involved in a decision?
8. Is there a target date or practical constraint we should plan around?

## Close

Summarise the problem in the prospect's language, confirm the next step, owner, and date.

- Recommended next step: `[Audit / proposal / follow-up / no current fit]`
- Owner: `[Name]`
- Date: `[YYYY-MM-DD]`
