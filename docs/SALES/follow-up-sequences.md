# Follow-up Sequences

Use helpful, specific follow-ups and honour a prospect's stated preferences. Stop contact if they ask not to be contacted.

## After a discovery call

### Same day

Thank the prospect, summarise the agreed priorities and next step, and state when they can expect the proposal or information.

### After `[3–5 business days]`

Ask whether they have any questions about the proposal or the priorities discussed. Restate the agreed decision point.

### Final check-in

Offer a simple choice: proceed, adjust scope, pause, or close the conversation for now. Record the outcome.
