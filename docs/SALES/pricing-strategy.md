# Pricing Strategy

## Principles

- Start with the commercial problem and expected scope, not a generic feature list.
- Use approved starting-price guidance only; confirm final investment after discovery.
- State what is included, excluded, and dependent on client input.
- Avoid promising specific revenue, rankings, lead volume, or outcomes.

## Pricing conversation framework

1. Reconfirm the priority and desired outcome.
2. Explain the recommended scope and why it fits.
3. Present the investment alongside deliverables, timeline, and assumptions.
4. Check for concerns about scope, timing, or budget.
5. Agree a clear next step.
