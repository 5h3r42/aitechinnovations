# Sales Playbook

## Purpose

Guide a consistent, consultative sales process for UK service businesses. Focus on the prospect's commercial problem, a clear next step, and only services that genuinely fit.

## Sales stages

1. Research the business and record evidence-based observations.
2. Start a relevant conversation using an approved outreach message.
3. Qualify need, fit, decision process, timing, and budget context.
4. Run a focused discovery call.
5. Recommend a clear scope with the relevant demo or examples.
6. Send a tailored proposal and agree the next decision point.
7. Follow up respectfully and record every material interaction.
8. Onboard confirmed clients using the onboarding checklist.

## Qualification prompts

- What are you trying to improve in the next `[time period]`?
- How do new enquiries or bookings currently arrive?
- What happens after someone visits the website?
- Who is involved in the decision?
- Is there a timing, budget, or launch constraint we should account for?

## Record keeping

Update `docs/CRM/prospect-tracker.csv` and the relevant file in `docs/CLIENTS/` after each meaningful interaction.
